about.

My name is Dean Shaw and this is my portfolio. I am currently a student at the University of Washington studying Business with the hopes of pursing computer science. This page is meant to give insight into my creative projects and provide an alternate perspective of myself. Every piece of work found on this page was done by me including the site itself with the exception of the photo of the day. Ranging many mediums and styles, my portfolio is comprised of photos, drawings, paintings, videos, animations, and digital artwork. Most of these works were completed from 2019 - Present. I most enjoy creating projects that give a sense of a distored reality, combining sci-fi or futurisitc elements with everyday items. Thank you for viewing and I hope you enjoy.
